COVID-19 Guidelines

Due to COVID-19, HackBI IV will be following guidelines that have not been used at previous HackBI events. This page contains the COVID-19 guidelines that HackBI IV participants will be expected to follow.

General Hackathon Guidelines

Bishop Ireton follows the Virginia guidelines, Alexandria guidelines, and school guidelines

  1. Put 6 feet between you and other people whenever possible
  2. Properly wear a mask at all times
  3. Do not enter the school if you have COVID-19 symptoms
