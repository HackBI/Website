COVID-19 Guidelines


Due to COVID-19, HackBI IV will be following guidelines that have not been used at previous HackBI events. This page contains the COVID-19 guidelines that HackBI IV participants will be expected to follow.



Guidlines and Procedures Taken to Keep a Safe and Healthy Enviroment:
Maintain a distance of 6 feet from people at all times
Wear a mask at all times
Hand sanitizer and wet wipes are provided in every room
Food is pre-packaged
Each bathroom allows for only one person inside at a time
All workshop rooms will be santitized after each use
Seating charts for each room of participants will be kept and recorded
